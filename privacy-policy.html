Privacy Policy for Happy Hour Minnesota

Effective Date: July 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Happy Hour Minnesota ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application ("App").

1. Information We Collect

  • Email address and login details
  • Location data (to show nearby bars and deals)
  • Device and app usage analytics
  • Information bar owners provide for listings

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To personalize your experience and show local specials
  • To allow bar owners to manage their listings and specials
  • To send push notifications and important updates
  • To monitor app performance and fix bugs

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your personal data. Data may be shared with third-party services for functionality (e.g., push notifications or analytics), but only as needed.

4. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

5. Your Choices

You can disable location permissions or opt out of push notifications in your device settings. You may also delete your account at any time.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. If we make material changes, we will notify users via the app or email.

7. Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at: support@happyhourmn.com
